定义：L代表Layer层,$L_j$第j层
(1) $L_j= \sum_{i=1}^{n}\omega_{ij}L_{j-1}+\theta_j$
(2) $\varepsilon_j = \frac{1}{1+e^{-L_j}}\qquad$
(3) $C = {\frac{1}{2}}.{(T_j-\varepsilon_j)^2}$

证明1：
因为：${(\frac{1}{x})}^{‘} ={\frac{-1}{x^2}} $
所以：$\varepsilon_j^{‘}={\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}}}^{‘}={\frac{1}{(1+e^{-x})^2}}e^{-x}$
$={\frac{(1+e^{-x} -1)}{1+e^{-x}}}.{\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}}}$
$=(1-\varepsilon_j)\varepsilon_j$
由链式法则：
${\frac{\sigma_C}{\sigma_\omega}}={\frac{\sigma_C}{\varepsilon_j}}.{\frac{\varepsilon_j}{\sigma_\omega}}$
$={\frac{\sigma_C}{\varepsilon_j}}.{\frac{\varepsilon_j}{\varepsilon_{L_j}}}.{\frac{\varepsilon_{L_j}}{\sigma_\omega}}$

(1): $({\frac{1}{2}}(T_j-\varepsilon_j)^2)^{‘}=(T_j-\varepsilon_j)(\varepsilon_j)^{‘}=\varepsilon_j(1-\varepsilon_j)(T_j-\varepsilon_j)$
(2): 由证明1知 $(\varepsilon_j)^{‘}=\varepsilon_j(1-\varepsilon_j)$
(3): $L_j = \omega L_{j-1}+\theta_j$
所以：
${\frac{\sigma_C}{\sigma_\omega}}=(T_j-\omega_j){\omega_j}(1-\omega_j)L_{j-1}$
${\frac{\sigma_C}{\sigma_\theta}}=(T_j-\omega_j){\omega_j}(1-\omega_j)$
